# Royal Phnom Penh Hospital - Patient Services Guide

## International Patient Services

Royal Phnom Penh Hospital is proud to offer comprehensive services for international patients seeking quality healthcare in Cambodia. Our International Patient Center provides personalized assistance to ensure a seamless medical journey.

### Services for International Patients:
- Pre-arrival consultations via video conference
- Airport transportation arrangements
- Medical visa assistance
- Interpreter services in 12 languages
- Accommodation arrangements for family members
- International insurance coordination
- Medical record translation
- Post-treatment follow-up care coordination

## Specialized Treatment Programs

### Cardiac Rehabilitation Program
- Duration: 6-12 weeks
- Services: Supervised exercise, nutrition counseling, stress management
- Director: Dr. Sopheak Rith
- Requirements: Referral from cardiologist
- Schedule: Monday, Wednesday, Friday (Morning and Afternoon sessions)

### Diabetes Management Center
- Services: Blood glucose monitoring, insulin management, lifestyle education
- Specialists: Dr. Chantha Heng (Endocrinologist), Ms. Srey Oun (Diabetes Educator)
- Operating Hours: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- Special Services: Continuous glucose monitoring system fitting
- Group Classes: Tuesdays and Thursdays, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM

### Women's Health Center
- Services: Obstetrics, gynecology, fertility treatments, menopause management
- Director: Dr. Lisa Kumar (Gynecologist)
- Operating Hours: Monday-Saturday, 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- Special Programs: Prenatal classes, breastfeeding support
- Appointment Booking: women.health@royalphnompenhhospital.com.kh

### Sleep Disorders Clinic
- Services: Sleep studies, CPAP therapy, insomnia treatment
- Specialist: Dr. Maria Santos (Neurologist with Sleep Medicine certification)
- Testing Hours: Monday-Thursday evenings, 8:00 PM - 6:00 AM
- Consultations: Fridays, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- Insurance Coverage: Most international health plans accepted

## Hospital Facilities

### Accommodation Options
- VIP Suites (10 rooms): Private nurse, family accommodation, dining services
- Private Rooms (50 rooms): En-suite bathroom, TV, WiFi, visitor sofa
- Semi-Private (30 rooms): Two beds per room, shared bathroom, TV, WiFi
- General Ward: 4-6 beds per room, central monitoring system

### Dining Services
- Patient Meal Options: Standard, Vegetarian, Halal, Diabetic, Low-sodium
- Cafeteria Hours: 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM daily
- Room Service: Available for VIP and Private rooms (6:00 AM - 8:00 PM)
- Visitor Meal Passes: Available at reception
